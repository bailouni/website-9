About Me

Emotion into experience.

Rayan Bailouni is a Billboard-charting music producer from Dubai, known for crafting records that sit at the intersection of emotional depth and cinematic intensity. Blending Western and Middle Eastern influences, his sound moves between raw vulnerability and high-impact production — creating music that feels both personal and larger than life.

With over 500 credits and more than 1.2 billion streams across his catalog, he has worked with artists including J Hus, Saint Levant, NLE Choppa, Bayou, King P Money, Noel Kharman and contributed to the first-ever Fire in the Booth with Charlie Sloth in Dubai for BBC Radio. In 2020, he produced the official Rally Dakar theme track for Saudi Arabia, alongside composing for global brands such as Nescafé, Adidas, Amazon, McDonald’s, BBC, TikTok, and Red Bull.

But beyond the credits, Bailouni’s work is rooted in something deeper. He creates music to explore themes of identity, longing, self-blame and transformation. His records don’t just tell stories; they build immersive worlds where artists and listeners alike are pushed to confront the parts of themselves they’ve been avoiding.

Whether in the studio or across his solo work, his focus remains the same: turning emotion into experience, and sound into self-discovery.
